The map above shows a plot of locations of fastfood restaurants, each color corresponds to a specfic chain.

  • Yellow: Mcdonalds

A clear pattern emerges from the plot; the majority of fastfood restaurants are on the east-side of the Country.

According to [13] the East Coast states alone account for about 36% of the United States' total population. Thus it is no surprice that the vast majority of fastfood restaurants are located for ease of access to the majority of the population.

Looking at the restaurants on the West Coast, it is very clear that is within the major cities of i.e Washington that the restaurants are distributed. Likewise a desert state like Nevada - Las Vegas accounts for a visible distribution on the State map!
